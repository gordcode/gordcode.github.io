Method of Loci

As part of my Professional Practices course I was given the option to do an alternative assignment. I had always been curious about memory techniques and often wondered how effective thay were. Anyone who has experience with Linux knows that there is no easy way to remember Linux commands! So I decided to try using the Method of Loci to see if it would help me memorize 80 commands from Linux. What can I say except that I was blown away at the effectivness of the technique!
