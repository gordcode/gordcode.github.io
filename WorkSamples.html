Energy Olympics Song - Michelin

When I worked at Michelin a competition was held by the company offering prizes to whatever team had the best energy saving idea for the factory. A few departments had their ideas implemented by the company. The team I was on decided to take a more musical approach by trying to raise awareness through song. We didn't save the company any money, but we had a great time doing it and were awarded a complementary basket full of goodies!
